Our Story

WIC Coffee started from a childhood memory among fruit trees and liberica coffee plants. What began as curiosity turned into a passion for roasting and brewing exceptional coffee.


Today WIC Café has won multiple awards including Barista Champion, Coffee Art Champion, and the SMH Good Café Guide Best Coffee award.
